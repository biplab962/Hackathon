Developing a culture of engaging
with science in Nepal.
Engage Nepal with Science Works (ENSW) is a pioneering initiative dedicated to fostering scientific literacy and innovation across Nepal. Rooted in the belief that science and technology are crucial drivers of progress, ENSW seeks to empower individuals, particularly the youth, with the knowledge and skills needed to tackle the challenges of the 21st century.
At its core, ENSW operates as a multifaceted platform, integrating various elements such as educational outreach, research collaboration, and community engagement. Through dynamic programs and initiatives, ENSW aims to democratize access to science education, bridging the gap between urban and rural communities. By organizing workshops, seminars, and hands-on activities, ENSW strives to ignite curiosity and passion for science among Nepali youth, inspiring the next generation of scientists, engineers, and innovators.
